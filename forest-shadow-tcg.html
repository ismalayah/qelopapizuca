The Forest Shadow Theme Deck from the Sun & Moon expansion of the Pokémon Trading Card Game predominantly focuses on Grass-type Pokémon. Each Theme Deck is packaged with a 60-card deck, damage counters, a custom coin, a rulesheet, a card list, a deckbox, a two-player mat, and a TCGO code card.

Blurb

Your Adventure in a New Region Starts Now!

Moving through the trees with ultimate stealth: Decidueye! This hard-striking deck paints a target on your foe—and hits the bull's-eye. Take aim with the Forest Shadow theme deck!

Trivia

  • The Cracked Ice Holofoil version of Decidueye is exclusive to this deck.
  • The Non Holofoil versions of Shiinotic and Kangaskhan are exclusive to this deck.
