Tragic Accident Claims Lives of Two 16-Year-Olds from Thibodaux

 Posted on July 18, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  186 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Thibodaux, Louisiana – In a devastating incident, two 16-year-olds lost their lives after their car crashed into a culvert just off LA 1.

According to Louisiana State Police, the driver, identified as Tristan Hartman, was unable to maintain control of the vehicle while negotiating a curve. The car veered off the road, colliding with a culvert. Despite emergency responders’ efforts, both Tristan and his passenger, Ashton Newkirk, succumbed to their injuries at the scene.

Another passenger, a 14-year-old, sustained life-threatening injuries and was transported to an undisclosed medical facility for further treatment.

The community is devastated by this tragic event, as two vibrant young individuals were taken far too soon. Our hearts go out to the families and friends of Tristan and Ashton, who are grieving the loss of their loved ones.

As the investigation continues, it serves as a stark reminder of the importance of safe driving practices, particularly for young drivers. Let us come together to support one another during this difficult time and strive for safer roads for all.
