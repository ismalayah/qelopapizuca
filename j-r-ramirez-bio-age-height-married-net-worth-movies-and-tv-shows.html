J. R. Ramirez Biography

J. R. Ramirez is a Cuban-American actor well known for his series regular role of Detective Jared Vasquez on the NBC series Manifest. Before, he was a series regular and beloved fan favorite as Julio on Starz series Power and Oscar Arocho on Netflix series Jessica Jones produced by Marvel. Ramirez also played Ted Grant / Wildcat in a recurring role in the third season of Arrow.

J. R. Ramirez Age

J. R. Ramirez  was born on October 8, 1980 in Matanzas, Cuba. He is 38 years old as of 2019

J. R. Ramirez Married | Spouse | Wife

Ramirez is not married and has no wife. He had a girlfriend in 2012, named Adrienne Bailon and they were together in The Coalition. But they separated the same year.

J. R. Ramirez Net Worth

Ramirez has an estimated net worth of $1 million. Acting is the primary source of his income.

J. R. Ramirez Early Life

Born in Matanzas Cuba, Ramirez moved to the United States, when he was a baby with his family and he was raised in Tampa, Florida.

J. R. Ramirez Career

On the first season of Power, Ramirez played a recurring character before being promoted to a regular cast member in the second season. He followed up by having a recurring appearance as Ted Grant / Wildcat on the third season of Arrow, prior to joining the second season of Jessica Jones as Oscar Arocho. In February 2018, he was cast as Detective Jared Vasquez in the NBC series Manifest.
