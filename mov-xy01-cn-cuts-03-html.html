Dogasu's Backpack | Episode Comparisons | Movies When Cartoon Network USA aired of Pokémon the Movie Diancie and the Cocoon of Destruction on November 8th, 2014, about five minutes' worth of footage was cut to make the movie short enough to air in the allotted time slot.  This page documents those cuts.

It is assumed that this removed footage will be restored for the DVD release.





Cut - 4 seconds
An establishing shot of the gang on the road gets cut immediately after the cut Argus / Millis scene.




Cut - 12 seconds
Some establishing shots of the camp that Satoshi and his friends have set up as well as a cute little scene of Satoshi and Pikachu are all removed from the TV edit.



Cut - 8 seconds
An eight-second scene of Eureka thanking Diancie for letting her keep the diamond it makes is cut from the TV broadcast.




"Thank you, Diancie."
*laughs*

Cut - 9 seconds
The last nine seconds of the girls' shopping get cut.  These show off a number of new costumes and an adorable scene of Eureka swimming on the floor of the dressing room.



Cut - 7 seconds
Uschi, the character created for the sole reason of giving Uchida Atsuto a character to voice in this movie, gets cut out of the TV broadcast.



Cut - 2 seconds
Citron's theatrical reveal of his ladder invention is two seconds longer in the theatrical version.



"Leave it up to me!"
